Verify: GBP dashboard shows additional categories beyond your primary. These expand the query types you're eligible to appear in.
HIGH

Section 2 — Schema Markup (5 items)
LocalBusiness schema on homepage
Verify: Go to search.google.com/test/rich-results, enter your homepage URL. Schema is detected with zero critical errors.
HIGH
Schema type is specific (not generic "LocalBusiness")
Verify: Your @type is the most specific applicable value: Restaurant, LegalService, MedicalBusiness, etc. Check schema.org for your correct type.
HIGH
FAQPage schema on About or FAQ page
Verify: Rich Results Test on your About/FAQ page detects FAQPage schema. At least 4 questions covering services, location, hours, and contact.
HIGH
Schema NAP matches GBP exactly
Verify: Business name, address, and phone in your schema are character-for-character identical to your GBP listing. No abbreviation differences.
HIGH
sameAs field links to all major profiles
Verify: Your schema's sameAs array includes URLs to your Yelp, Facebook, and GBP profiles at minimum. These links connect your entity across platforms.
MED

/facts page created and published

Section 5 — Reviews & Authority (4 items)
25+ Google reviews at 4.2+ average
Verify: Google Maps shows your star count and review total. This is the threshold where AI systems begin treating you as confidently recommendable.
HIGH
Reviews present on at least 2 platforms (Google + one other)
Verify: You have reviews on Google AND at least one of: Yelp, Facebook, or an industry-specific review site. Multi-platform review presence strengthens credibility signals.
MED
At least one editorial mention from an independent source
Verify: A local news site, trade publication, chamber website, or industry blog mentions your business with a link. Not a paid ad. Not a press release you self-published.
MED
Industry association membership listed on their directory
Verify: Your primary trade association's member directory shows your business. These domain-authority signals carry strong weight in AI training data.
