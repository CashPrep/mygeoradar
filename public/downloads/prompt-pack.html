How to use this pack: For each prompt, replace the placeholders in [brackets] with your business details. Run each prompt in the AI assistants listed on the card. Screenshot every response and compare against your last audit. The goal isn’t a perfect score — it’s knowing exactly where you stand so you can prioritize.

Prompt 01
Entity Awareness Check
Run first
ChatGPT Perplexity Gemini Claude
Tell me everything you know about [Business Name] in [City, State]. Include their address, phone number, website, services, hours, and anything else you have on file.
🔍 What to look for:

Does the AI know your business exists? Is the information accurate? Are key details missing or wrong? Note every incorrect fact.

⚡ What to do with it:

Any wrong or missing fact becomes a priority fix. Update GBP, schema, and your /facts page to correct it. Re-run in 30 days.

Prompt 02
Recommendation Position Check
Most important
ChatGPT Perplexity Gemini Claude
I'm in [City] and I need a [service/business type]. Which specific businesses would you recommend and why?
🔍 What to look for:

Are you mentioned? Where in the list? What reasons does the AI give for recommending others? Are any facts about competitors wrong (potential opportunity)?

⚡ What to do with it:

If you’re not mentioned at all, entity and citation work is your priority. If you’re mentioned but low, credibility signals (reviews, editorial mentions) need work.

Prompt 03
Facts Accuracy Audit
ChatGPT Perplexity Gemini Claude
For [Business Name] in [City]: what is their exact street address, phone number, website URL, business hours, and the full list of services they offer?
🔍 What to look for:

Pinpoint factual errors. AI confidently stating wrong information is a hallucination — needs to be corrected with authoritative signal flooding.

⚡ What to do with it:

For each wrong fact: correct on GBP, all directories, your schema, and your /facts page. If multiple AIs agree on a wrong fact, add a press mention with the correct info.

Prompt 04
Reputation & Sentiment Check
ChatGPT Perplexity Gemini
What do customer reviews and online reputation say about [Business Name] in [City]? Summarize what people commonly praise and what complaints exist.
🔍 What to look for:

Is the sentiment positive? Does AI know you have reviews at all? Are any fabricated complaints being reported? Is the summary accurate?

⚡ What to do with it:

If AI says “I don’t have review data,” you need more reviews on more platforms. If sentiment is wrong, address the underlying review issues and ensure positive reviews are recent.

Prompt 05
Competitor Gap Analysis
Strategic
ChatGPT Perplexity Gemini
Compare [Business Name] to 3 other [business type] businesses in [City]. Which would you recommend and why? What does each do best?
🔍 What to look for:

Who are your AI-perceived competitors? What attributes is the AI using to rank them? What are they described as doing better than you?

⚡ What to do with it:

The attributes used to praise competitors are your gap list. If a competitor is praised for “faster response time,” build content and review signals around that attribute.

Prompt 06
Content Gap Discovery
ChatGPT Gemini Claude
What questions do people most commonly ask when choosing a [business type] in [City or region]? List at least 10 specific questions.
🔍 What to look for:

Every question the AI lists is a content opportunity. Does your website answer these questions clearly? Does your FAQ schema include them?

⚡ What to do with it:

For any question your website doesn’t clearly answer, add it to your FAQ page and FAQPage schema. These questions are exactly what AI is being asked about your category.

Prompt 07
Local Authority Opportunities
ChatGPT Gemini Claude
What local organizations, publications, directories, events, or associations in [City] would be most relevant for a [business type] to get listed in or mentioned by?
🔍 What to look for:

Specific names of local platforms, associations, and publications you may have overlooked. These are your next citation and editorial mention targets.

⚡ What to do with it:

Add any you haven’t pursued to your outreach list. Prioritize ones the AI mentions multiple times — that signals they’re weighted sources for your category.

Prompt 08
Schema & Structured Data Review
Technical
ChatGPT Claude
Here is the LocalBusiness schema markup from my website. Review it for missing fields, incorrect values, or improvements I should make to maximize AI visibility: [Paste your full JSON-LD schema here]
🔍 What to look for:

Missing properties (geo coordinates, areaServed, sameAs links, openingHoursSpecification), incorrect @type, incomplete address, missing priceRange.

⚡ What to do with it:

Implement every suggested fix, then re-validate at search.google.com/test/rich-results. This prompt works especially well in Claude — paste your raw JSON and it will return detailed, actionable feedback.

Prompt 09
About Page AI Readability Audit
ChatGPT Claude
Here is the About page copy from my [business type] website. Review it as if you were an AI assistant trying to learn about this business. What key facts are missing, unclear, or buried? What would make this page more likely to be cited as a source? [Paste your About page text here]
🔍 What to look for:

Vague statements, missing facts (founding year, location, team, licenses), promotional language that lacks substance, missing FAQs, buried credentials.

⚡ What to do with it:

Rewrite your About page addressing every gap identified. Then re-run Prompt 01 in 30 days to see if AI’s description of you has improved.

Prompt 10
Quarterly Weakness Summary
Run last
ChatGPT Perplexity Gemini Claude
Based on everything you know about [Business Name] in [City, State], what are the top 3 weaknesses in their online presence that would make you less confident recommending them compared to competitors?
🔍 What to look for:

Specific, actionable gaps the AI identifies — “limited review presence,” “unclear service area,” “no mentions in local press.” These are your top priorities for the next quarter.

⚡ What to do with it:

Screenshot the responses from all four AIs side-by-side. Any weakness mentioned by 2 or more is a consensus priority. Make it your #1 focus for the next 30 days.
