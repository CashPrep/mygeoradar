# The 27-Point AI Visibility Checklist
**Found by AI — MyGeoRadar**

---

> **How to use:** Work through this checklist after reading the full playbook. Each item is a concrete, binary action — either done or not done. No partial credit. Check it off only when fully complete.

---

## SECTION A: Google Business Profile (6 points)

- [ ] **A1.** GBP claimed and verified (postcard, phone, or video)
- [ ] **A2.** Primary category is the most specific option available for your business type
- [ ] **A3.** At least 3 secondary categories added
- [ ] **A4.** Business description is 250+ words, written in complete sentences, mentions all main services and your location
- [ ] **A5.** At least 10 photos uploaded (logo, exterior, interior, team, products/services)
- [ ] **A6.** All services and products added with descriptions

---

## SECTION B: Reviews & Reputation (3 points)

- [ ] **B1.** At least 25 Google reviews with an average of 4.5+ stars
- [ ] **B2.** Every review (positive and negative) has been responded to within 48 hours
- [ ] **B3.** Active review presence on at least one additional platform (Yelp, Facebook, or industry-specific)

---

## SECTION C: NAP Consistency (2 points)

- [ ] **C1.** Business name, address, and phone are identical on Google, Yelp, Facebook, and your website
- [ ] **C2.** Website URL is correct and consistent across all platforms (www vs. non-www, http vs. https)

---

## SECTION D: Website Foundations (4 points)

- [ ] **D1.** Homepage first 200 words clearly state: business name, what you do, where, and who you serve
- [ ] **D2.** About page rewritten using the quotable paragraph formula from the playbook
- [ ] **D3.** A /facts or key information section exists with: founding year, founder name, services, certifications, associations
- [ ] **D4.** Website loads on HTTPS (not HTTP) — no SSL warning in browser

---

## SECTION E: Technical SEO & Crawlability (3 points)

- [ ] **E1.** Google Search Console set up and sitemap submitted
- [ ] **E2.** Bing Webmaster Tools set up and sitemap submitted
- [ ] **E3.** robots.txt does not block Googlebot, Bingbot, or GPTBot

---

## SECTION F: Schema Markup (4 points)

- [ ] **F1.** LocalBusiness (or specific subtype) JSON-LD schema on homepage — validated with no errors at validator.schema.org
- [ ] **F2.** Organization schema on homepage if national/online business
- [ ] **F3.** FAQPage schema on at least 2 pages with real questions customers ask
- [ ] **F4.** AggregateRating included in schema with current accurate review count and rating

---

## SECTION G: Directory Citations (3 points)

- [ ] **G1.** Submitted and complete on: Bing Places, Apple Business Connect, Yelp, BBB, Facebook, LinkedIn
- [ ] **G2.** Submitted to at least 3 secondary directories (Yellow Pages, Foursquare, Manta, Superpages, Hotfrog)
- [ ] **G3.** Submitted to top industry-specific directory for your business type

---

## SECTION H: Content & Credibility (2 points)

- [ ] **H1.** At least 1 piece of content published in the last 60 days that answers a specific customer question
- [ ] **H2.** At least 1 third-party editorial mention of your business exists (local news, industry publication, community organization)

---

## SCORING

**24–27 points:** Excellent — you're in the top tier of AI-visible businesses. Run the prompt pack monthly and maintain.

**18–23 points:** Good — you're being mentioned on some assistants. Focus on the unchecked items in order, starting with Foundation sections A and D.

**12–17 points:** Below average — significant gaps exist. Work through sections in order: A → D → F → G → B → C → E → H.

**Under 12 points:** Not yet visible to AI. Start at A1 and work through every item. Within 30–60 days of completing all 27, you will be visible.

---

## QUICK WIN PRIORITY ORDER

If you're short on time, do these 7 items first — they have the highest impact per hour invested:

1. A4 — Rewrite your GBP description (30 minutes)
2. D2 — Rewrite your About page (1 hour)
3. F1 — Add LocalBusiness schema to your homepage (45 minutes)
4. F3 — Add FAQPage schema to 2 pages (1 hour)
5. G1 — Submit to Bing Places and Apple Business Connect (30 minutes)
6. E2 — Set up Bing Webmaster Tools (20 minutes)
7. B1 — Send review request emails to your last 20 customers (30 minutes)

**Total time for quick wins: ~4.5 hours. Expected impact: measurable improvement within 30 days.**
